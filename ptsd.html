PTSD Assist

smartphone app // network of support

Getting the necessary support to treat PTSD can be difficult, and many sufferers are left undiagnosed and untreated. With PTSD assist, we aim to educate firefighters on its symptoms, and provide lines of communication to veteran firefighters who can guide them through the recovery process.

 
 

Project Summary

This was the final project of the IAT 333 Interaction Design Methods course. Our goal was to integrate ourselves into an established group and to design something that can enhance their lives in some meaningful way.

We were able to find a team of firefighters willing to let us observe and meet with them to discuss particulars about their experiences on the job.

For 13 weeks we met with our participant group, further refining and iterating our design focus each week. My partner and I eventually realized there was a serious lack in PTSD treatment and focused our final design towards remedying that.
