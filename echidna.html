Echidna

wearable technology // emotions personified

Echidna is an empathetic dress that is able to detect its wearer's emotions and react accordingly. It was built using the Arduino rapid-prototyping and wiring system, thread and cloth, and lots of love.

 
 

Project Summary

This was the final project of the IAT 320 Body Interface course. We were tasked with creating an artifact that satisfied the principles of affective computing.

My partner and I decided that we would focus our efforts in the field of fashion technology. Due to her experience with graphic design and arts and crafts, and my technical expertise*, the roles were very clearly defined.

Over the course of 10 weeks, we sketched, iterated, and presented our work to a group of peers as part of the refinement process. Ultimately, our efforts culminated in the creation of Echidna, shown below.
